Automatically evaluate the quality of your store against a 153 point checklist. Identify poor product images, catch missing descriptions, benchmark your prices against competitors. It's like employing a mystery shopper, 24x7. Except, better.

Things we check.

We know that each store is different but there's a few things that all customers expect when shopping online. That's why our 153 checks include:

  • Return policy available and prominent.
  • Delivery timeframe displayed.
  • Delivery price (even if estimated) is displayed.
  • Return policy available and prominent.
  • Related or similar products displayed.
  • Specifications are listed, where appropriate.
  • No popovers covering the store content.
  • Compliance banners are present and correctly sized.
  • Product images are of good quality.
  • Enough product image are provided to give a complete view.
  • Stock information is available and prominent
  • Pages load quickly
  • OpenGraph tags are present for best social sharing experience.
  • Store contact details are provided to customers.
